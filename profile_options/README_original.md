### [EthnoGen Nexus (v2.1)](https://github.com/Arshdeep2k5/ethnogen_V2.0)
*An on-device, multi-target virtual screening platform linking 3D Graph Neural Networks, physical molecular docking, and 3-step forward retrosynthesis to discover drug leads from traditional botanical libraries.*

```text

                                                                │
[ Commited to DB ]   ◄── [ S-8: Synthesis ] ◄── [ S-7: Vina ] ◄─┼── [ S-6: 3D-EGNN ]
```

<details>
<summary><b>🔍 INSPECT SYSTEM COMPONENT SPECIFICATIONS (Click to Expand)</b></summary>
<br>

*   **S-0 (PhytoFormer):** A character-level SMILES GPT Transformer decoder utilizing Rotary Positional Embeddings (RoPE) and Morgan Tanimoto similarity distance penalties to generate diverse chemical derivatives.
*   **S-1 to S-4.5 (CPU Safety):** Ruthlessly filters out candidates based on PAINS alerts, QED drug-likeness, 2D hERG cardiotoxicity, CYP450 liver stability, and Delaney ESOL solubility.
*   **S-5 (Conformation):** Generates 3D coordinates constrained to computed target pocket anchors using RDKit's ETKDGv3 and MMFF94 energy minimization.
*   **S-6 (The AI Judge):** A 3D E(n)-Equivariant Graph Neural Network (EGNN) that predicts sub-atomic target binding affinity ($pK_d$).
*   **S-7 (Vina):** Executes on-device, C++ physical docking simulations (including native metalloprotein routing to AutoDock4-Zn).
*   **S-8 (Retrosynthesis):** Enforces a strict $\le 3$-step synthesizability recipe starting directly from our natural seed library using Late-Stage Functionalization (LSF) SMARTS templates.
